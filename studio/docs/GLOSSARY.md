## Core Terms

### Event
A scheduled Aurrin program session (for example, a pitch night) where founders present, judges score, and operations workflows run.

### Founder
A startup applicant or accepted participant who submits a company profile, pitch details, and supporting assets.

### Judge
A scorer assigned to an event who evaluates founder pitches using a rubric and submits weighted or category scores.

### Mentor
A contributor matched to founders to provide tactical guidance and follow-up support after review cycles.

### Rubric
A structured scoring framework made of categories, criteria, and point ranges that standardize judging.

### Rubric Template
The reusable base definition used to produce immutable rubric versions for live events.

### Rubric Version
A frozen rubric snapshot tied to an event so all submitted scores use the same scoring definition.

### Score Lock
The control that prevents further edits to submitted scores once judging is finalized.

### Publishing Window
The period when locked results are released to founders and downstream reporting channels.

### Founder Application
The intake record submitted through the public application flow before acceptance and assignment.

### Founder Pitch
An event-specific record connecting a founder, pitch content, and scoring lifecycle.

### Audience Session
A time-bounded validation session that allows audience participants to submit feedback responses.

### Entitlement
An access grant created by subscription or purchase that controls premium feature access.

### Outbox Job
A durable background task record used for async work such as email, PDF generation, and webhooks.

### Audit Trail
Immutable records of sensitive actions (role changes, score publication, approvals, and operational overrides).

## Acronyms

### RLS
Row-Level Security. PostgreSQL policy enforcement that restricts row access by actor and scope.

### RBAC
Role-Based Access Control. Authorization model that maps users to roles and scoped permissions.

### RMS
Rubric Management System. Internal term for rubric template/version lifecycle and score model governance.

### MRR
Monthly Recurring Revenue. Subscription revenue normalized to a monthly value.

### ARR
Annual Recurring Revenue. Subscription revenue normalized to a 12-month value.

### KPI
Key Performance Indicator. A measurable signal used for operations and growth monitoring.

### TTL
Time To Live. Expiration window for generated tokens, signed URLs, or temporary resources.
