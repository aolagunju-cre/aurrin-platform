# Founder Application Guide

## How-to: Submit your application

1. Visit `/public/apply`.
2. Complete required fields:
   - full name
   - email
   - company name
   - pitch summary
   - industry
   - stage
3. Upload your pitch deck as a PDF (max 50 MB).
4. Optionally add website, Twitter, and LinkedIn.
5. Submit the form.

## How-to: Prepare a valid submission

1. Keep pitch summary between 100 and 1000 characters.
2. Use a valid email address format.
3. Ensure deck file is a PDF under size limit.
4. Avoid multiple resubmissions inside 24 hours unless requested.

## What happens next

- Application is saved for admin review with initial status `pending`.
- A confirmation email job is queued after successful submission.
- Admins can transition status through `pending -> accepted -> assigned` (or `declined`).
- On `accepted`, founder user/profile provisioning is triggered and approval email is queued.

## FAQ

### When will I hear back?
The current intake flow targets response within 5 business days.

### Can I edit my application after submitting?
Self-service editing is not yet fully available through a dedicated portal flow.

### What if my upload fails?
Retry with a PDF under 50 MB and confirm required fields are complete.
