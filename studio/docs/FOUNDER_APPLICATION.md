# Founder Application Guide

## How to submit

1. Visit `/public/apply`.
2. Complete required fields: full name, email, company name, pitch summary, industry, stage.
3. Upload your pitch deck as a PDF (maximum 50 MB).
4. Optionally include website, Twitter, and LinkedIn links.
5. Submit the form.

## Expected response time

Aurrin reviews applications and contacts founders within 5 business days.

## What happens next

- Your application is stored for admin-only review with initial status `pending`.
- A confirmation email job is queued after successful submission.
- Admins can progress status through `pending -> accepted -> assigned` (or `declined`) using the protected approval API.
- On `accepted`, a founder user/profile is provisioned and a confirmation email is queued; founders must confirm email before platform access.

## Contact

For questions, contact the Aurrin Ventures team through official support channels.
